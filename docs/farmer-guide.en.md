# Kisan Saathi Farmer Guide (English)

This guide helps farmers use Kisan Saathi for daily crop decisions.

## What You Can Use

- Crop disease photo diagnosis
- Live mandi prices
- Weather forecast and farm advisories
- Government scheme information
- Notification alerts for important updates

## How to Use (Step by Step)

1. Open the app home page.
2. Choose your preferred language (Hindi/English).
3. Select the feature you need:
   - Crop Diagnosis
   - Mandi Prices
   - Weather
   - Govt Schemes
4. Enter your crop/location details.
5. Read the advisory and compare with local conditions.
6. For high-risk decisions, confirm with local agri officers or experts.

## Crop Advice: Safe Usage Rules

- Do not spray chemicals in strong wind or expected rain.
- Follow label dosage exactly for pesticides/fungicides.
- Use gloves, mask, and basic safety gear.
- Keep children and livestock away during spraying.
- Recheck diagnosis with fresh images if symptoms change.

## Weather Usage Tips

- Check 24-48 hour rain chance before irrigation.
- Plan spray in low-wind windows.
- Avoid spraying when rain probability is high.

## Mandi Price Usage Tips

- Compare price and transport cost together.
- Check nearby mandis before selling.
- Avoid distress selling on low-price days when storage is possible.

## Govt Scheme Usage Tips

- Verify deadlines on official websites.
- Keep Aadhaar, bank details, and land records ready.
- Use helpline numbers for application support.

## Important Note

Kisan Saathi provides AI-assisted guidance.
It is not a replacement for on-field agronomy inspection.
For severe disease, pest outbreaks, or major investment decisions, consult a local expert.
